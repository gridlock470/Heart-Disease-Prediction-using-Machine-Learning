How it works

  • • Each patient gets a unique QR code on registration.
  • • The QR encodes the patient's ID for quick access.
  • • Point your webcam at the patient's QR code to scan it.
  • • The system opens their secure medical history page.

When analyzing reports

While reviewing a patient's report, scan their QR code to instantly pull up their complete medical history, including past reports and uploaded medical documents.

Security

Only authenticated doctors can scan and view patient records. The QR code is doctor-exclusive and routes to a secure endpoint.
